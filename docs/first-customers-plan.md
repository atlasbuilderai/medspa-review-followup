# MedSpa Review Follow-Up — First Customers Plan

## Goal
Get the first 5 paying customers without ads.

## Acquisition Channels
### 1. Direct outreach
- med spa owners
- med spa managers
- med spa marketing leads

### 2. Agencies serving med spas
- boutique marketing agencies
- local SEO agencies
- med spa growth consultants

### 3. Niche communities
- Facebook groups
- med spa business/operator communities
- local marketing groups

## Offer Angle
- more Google reviews
- simpler than Podium/Birdeye
- lower monthly cost
- easy setup
- built for med spas specifically

## Founding Offer
- early adopter pricing
- concierge onboarding if needed
- feedback-driven product shaping

## First Outreach Message Angle
"If your med spa wants more Google reviews but doesn’t need a bloated $300/month reputation suite, I’m building a simpler review follow-up tool specifically for med spas. Interested in seeing the early version?"

## What To Validate
- do med spas understand the problem immediately?
- do they already use something else?
- do they care more about price or simplicity?
- is SMS necessary at first, or is email enough for early validation?
