# MedSpa Review Follow-Up — Guided Tour Copy

## Step 1 — Welcome
Welcome to MedSpa Review Follow-Up. This guided preview shows how a med spa can upload recent clients, send review requests, follow up automatically, and keep the workflow simple.

## Step 2 — Review Link
Start by saving your Google review link so every request points clients to the right destination.

## Step 3 — Contacts
Upload recent clients manually or by CSV. The product is built to make review follow-up easy without a bloated setup process.

## Step 4 — Campaign
Create a review request campaign for post-visit follow-up. Keep the workflow simple and reusable.

## Step 5 — Message
Customize the message template so it sounds like your med spa and fits your brand tone.

## Step 6 — Follow-Up
Schedule one follow-up after a few days to catch more responses without staff needing to remember manually.

## Step 7 — Results
Track contacts imported, requests sent, follow-ups sent, clicks, and overall campaign activity in one lightweight dashboard.

## Step 8 — Reputation Snapshot
See your Google rating, total reviews, and growth trend so the product feels tied to actual reputation outcomes, not just message sending.

## Step 9 — Templates
Start with med spa-specific request templates so the workflow feels faster to adopt and easier to trust.
