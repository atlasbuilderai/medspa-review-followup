# MedSpa Review Follow-Up — Pricing & Unit Economics

## Recommended Initial Pricing
### Best starting point
- Founding plan: **$39/month**

### Alternative lower-friction test
- Early beta plan: **$29/month**

## Why This Range
- much cheaper than Podium/Birdeye-style platforms
- still meaningful recurring revenue
- med spas are less price-sensitive than many lower-ticket service businesses
- room to absorb modest SMS and hosting costs

## Basic Unit Economics
### Fixed-ish early costs
- hosting/db/auth/jobs: ~$20–60/month
- email: ~$0–20/month
- misc tools/monitoring: ~$0–30/month

### Variable costs
- SMS provider charges
- phone numbers / carrier fees
- higher usage by active customers

## Practical Target
At $39/month:
- 26 customers ≈ $1,014 MRR

At $29/month:
- 35 customers ≈ $1,015 MRR

## Recommendation
Use one simple plan first.
Only add tiers after seeing real usage patterns.
